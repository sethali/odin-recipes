When life give you lemons...

Throw an apple pie!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Basil
  • Tomatoe sauce
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • mushrooms
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. Saute ground beef and onions
  2. Add tomatoe sauce
  3. Let simmer for 15 min
  4. Add mushrooms and Spinach
  5. Enjoy!
